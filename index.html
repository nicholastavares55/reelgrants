Questions

Good to know

Is the grant info real?

Yes. The programme above is loaded from our live database of real, currently-tracked film funds (Sundance, Firelight, Catapult, SFFILM, IDA, Doc Society and more). We show the typical window when a fund is between cycles, and only flag "OPEN NOW" once we've re-verified it against the funder's own page. Accuracy is the whole point — every link goes straight to the source.

What does it cost?

The public programme and a weekly "newly opened" email are free. A low monthly plan unlocks personalized matches, per-deadline alerts, saved watches, and text alerts. Join the early list to lock in founding-member pricing.

Who is this for?

Independent filmmakers — documentary, narrative, screenwriting, animation, shorts — and especially emerging and first-feature directors hunting development, production, and finishing funds. A Pro plan is coming for grant consultants and film programs.

How will I get alerted?

Email during early access, with instant text alerts coming for members. Deadlines don't wait, so we build for "the moment it opens," not "next month's roundup."
