Yes — the tracker above is seeded with real, currently-tracked film funds (Sundance, Firelight, Catapult, SFFILM, IDA and more). We show the typical window when a fund is between cycles, and only flag "OPEN NOW" once we've re-verified it. Accuracy is the whole point.

The public tracker and a weekly "newly opened" email are free. A low monthly plan unlocks personalized matches, per-deadline alerts, saved watches, and text alerts. Join the early list to lock in founding-member pricing.

Independent filmmakers — documentary, narrative, screenwriting, animation, shorts — and especially emerging and first-feature directors hunting development, production, and finishing funds. A Pro plan is coming for grant consultants and film programs.

Email during early access, with instant text alerts coming for members. Deadlines don't wait, so we build for "the moment it opens," not "next month's roundup."
